      Contracting out explanation
      Most people were contracted out at some time in their working life. That meant your NI contributions for that time were lower or paid into another pension, for example a private or workplace pension.

      Your forecast shows an estimate of what you would have paid into your State Pension if you had not been contracted out. This is the Contracted Out pension Equivalent (COPE) figure, it is only for information, you do not need to add or take away this figure from your State Pension forecast.

      If you have a substantial COPE figure you may need more than 35 years of NI contributions to reach the full State Pension.
